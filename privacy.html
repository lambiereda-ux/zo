Privacy Policy

App: OTHMANE FATI CAR (package name: com.othmanefati.car)

This Privacy Policy explains how OTHMANE FATI CAR handles information when you use the OTHMANE FATI CAR Android application.

1. Data collection and use

We do not collect, store, or share personal data through the app.

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No name, email, phone number, photos, contacts, files, or payment data is collected in-app.
  • No analytics SDK and no advertising SDK are integrated in the app.

2. Device permissions

The app does not request sensitive Android runtime permissions such as location, camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, or storage access.

The app manifest does not declare the Android INTERNET permission.

3. Third-party services and map links

The app can open external map applications when you tap map-related buttons. Any information processed at that point is handled by the external map provider under its own privacy policy, not by OTHMANE FATI CAR.

4. Data retention and deletion

Because we do not collect personal data in-app, we do not retain personal data from app usage and there is no in-app account data to delete.

5. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when needed. Any update will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
